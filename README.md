LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

Research Objective

This project explores how top LinkedIn creators and B2B SaaS marketers build audiences, generate engagement, and create demand through organic content.

Scope

* Identify 10 leading experts in LinkedIn content strategy
* Collect LinkedIn posts and YouTube content
* Analyze common content frameworks and growth tactics
* Build a foundation for a future B2B SaaS content playbook
